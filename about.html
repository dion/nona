How I Started

I was out of college in new york city and had graduated with a design degree, I had an offline portfolio and an online portfolio and was sending out resumes to every company in the nyc area but wasn't getting any responses back.

Needless to say it was frustrating to not get responses back, even for unpaid internships. I tweaked the hell out of my resume and portfolio and went to networking events with people in the same industry but none of that even got me a job.

I did manage to land a job from the college job search office, I did print and some design work there, but after that job, it was still the same situation of sending out resumes and not getting any responses back.

I started my own company and offered full website solutions. From design all the way to a finished site. The great thing about these last two experiences is that it helped me to build out my portfolio and experience. (job tip #1, pick up projects to build out your portfolio and experience)

I left new york city and moved to the san francisco bay area in california, and everything changed there. The biggest change was that there was alot more jobs available in the bay area compared to nyc at the time, and the rest is to be continued...
